About Zezo App


Zezo is a Web application to help reading problems in all languages of the world (or mostly) Using this application you can write a text in which owns the problem of reading and Zezo is reading the text you word for word as fast as you specify While continuing to using the application will improve your reading of the language you are working on Where you can then test in reading by Zezo too! And discover your mistakes to improve reading. Do not forget to apply Zezo has put voice commands, where it becomes fully responsive voice commands and use the application without the need for a mouse


SERVICES

What Zezo offer to you


READ

Read to you a text that you choose it by word by word with any language of your choice

LEVELS

Listen in different levels (Slow --> Fast)

MEANING

Search by wikipedia to the meaning of a word during read or listen



LISTEN

Listen to you to a text that you choose it

TESTING

During the listen to you zezo will test you and give you advices depending on your scores

IMPROVE

Will be improved in reading the new language


RESPONSE

" Hey Zezo " Just say it and then talk with zezo to use the site without mouse!
